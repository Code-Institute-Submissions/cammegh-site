Licences

There are various licences available from Cammegh to complement Cammegh products, across roulette wheels, billboard displays and even multiple casinos.

 

This page includes information on the licencing and graphics for Spread-Bet, Lucky Symbols, Double Shot and Multiboard software.

Wheel Manager

Live Viewer shows the wheel's current status, including game state, rotor and ball speed.

Various wheel settings can be configured, such as the speed thresholds for game states.

Cammegh's Wheel Manager software for Windows provides a simple tool to unlock the full power of the Mercury 360 roulette wheel.

With Wheel Manager running on a service laptop or management PC, access is granted to the comprehensive game logs stored within the wheel's secure memory. Aspects of each game - such as rotor integrity, wheel level, erroneous ball and rotor speeds, game interruptions, and unexpected ball drops - can all be exported for printing, or viewed directly within the software, which employs a simple traffic light display to denote status.

Using a serial cable, service personnel can quickly connect to a wheel and diagnose faults or perform calibration procedures by following the on-screen instrcutions. Networked wheels can be connected to remotely for real-time status reporting.

PitBoss HQ

PitBoss HQ provides a convenient browser-based interface for wheel and table productivity analysis over a local- or wide-area network. Working in conjunction with the Cammegh Billboard Display System, PitBoss securely feeds comprehensive game and table activity data into a database for analysis and reporting via the PitBoss HQ web app.

Wheel speed and rotor direction are logged alongside drop, float, dealer ID and winning number, with options to export data so it can be used in 3rd party systems. Wheels can be tracked by serial number, to accommodate their relocation around the gaming floor, while the distribution of their rotor speeds and winning numbers can be displayed graphically within the PitBoss HQ web app. Thanks to its web-based architecture, PitBoss can even be used over a wide-area network to allow the integration of multiple casinos.

Multiboard - Roulette

Multiboard is a feature of the Billboard Display System that shows the results from multiple tables on one screena and updates in real time. This keeps players up to date with their table's results while they are playing elsewhere or taking a break.

 

Multiboard can be featured on its own (for example, mounted above the casino bar) or it can be integrated into the on-screen graphics of another Billboard ("Multiboard Mini").

Multiboard - Baccarat

In addition to Multiboard for Roulette, Multiboard can also be used to display Baccarat results from multiple tables. The traditional Chinese Main Road from each connected table is shown, along with table name, statistics and the current open/closed status. Trends can be highlighted with colours and effects in order to entice players in from other parts of the casino.

Spread-Bet Roulette

Spread-Bet Roulette transparently generates a bonus number from 0 to 72, enabling high payouts at an increased house edge.

Two concentric roulette number rings appear on the display, spinning in opposite directions. At 'No More Bets', both rings stop, randomly aligning the numbers.

The winning roulette number then determines which two numbers are added together to make the final Spread-Bet value. Players bet on the spread into which this value falls.

How to Play

Spread-Bets are placed at the beginning of each game alongside (or instead of) traditional roulette bets. The Billboard Display shows the winning value and spread. Bets are paid out and collected at the end of the game.

Requirements

A Cammegh Billboard with the appropriate Spread-Bet Roulette softare is required, along with a cloth featuring the 7 Spread-Bet spots.

Spread-Bet Baccarat

Spread-Bet Baccarat is a licenced side bet for Baccarat, where players are invited to bet on the sum of either the Player's or the Banker's final (winning) hand.

By respectfully building on the original game, Spread-Bet Baccarat adds variety for the player aand an increased house edge for the casino, all without affecting the experience for traditional players.

How to Play

If the player has placed a traditional Baccarat bet, he or she is invited to bet on up to six available Spreads, three on the Player's side and three on the Banker's side. Spread-Bets are placed at the same time as the player buys into the traditional game. Winning and losing wagers are settled at the end of the game.

Requirements

Spread-Bet Baccarat is dealt from an 8 deck shoe, with th cutting card at least one deck from the back. Betting spots are printed on the cloth, which must be in Cammegh Gold*

*CMYK: 21 60 100 7 / RGB: 190 115 40

Spread-Bet Blackjack

Spread-Bet Blackjack is a licenced side bet for Blackjack. Working seamlessly alongside the traditional game, Spread-Bet Blackjack gives each player three new betting spots, which allow wagers to be placed on the sum of their first two cards. By adding the chance to win on a low value hand, Spread-Bet Blackjack adds an exciting twist to the game at an increased house edge.

How to Play

Spread-Bets are placed once the player has bought into the main game and before dealing commences. Winning and losing Spread-Bets are paid out/collected before the first player decides whether to receive another card.

Requirements

The side bet can be incorporated into any Blackjack table that uses a continual card shuffler. No additional equipment is required other than a table layout that includes the pay table and betting spots for Spread-Bet Blackjack.

Rules

When calculating a player's Spread-Bet Blackjack total, Aces are equal to 1. An optional game rule states that a player's Spread-Bets are pushed if the player is dealt a "Royal Blackjack" (Ace + picture card), which reduces the house edge as shown below.

Lucky Symbols

Lucky Symbols is a simple yet captivating side bet for roulette that boasts an extraordinary scope for customisation. Bet spots are added to the roulette layout (each containing a bespoke Lucky Symbol) and a slot reel animation on the Billboard Display selects one symbol per game. Players that have placed bets on the winning symbol are paid at the corresponding odds.

The side bet is powered by Cammegh's GLI-approved Bonus Numbers feature, whose flexibility allows symbol odds and house edges to be fine-tuned to a wide range of values. With its potential for large payouts at an increased house edge, Lucky Symbols makes a valuable addition to the gaming floor.

Double Shot

Cammegh's DoubleShot side bet makes elegant use of Bonus Numbers to add a random bonus number to the game. The inclusion of this bonus number, generated without the need for a custom roulette wheel, allows players to bet on number matches at odds of 1200, 600 and 300 to 1 with an increased house edge.

With the inclusion of a secondary betting area on the dealer's side of the roulette layout and a slot reel animation on the Billboard Display, DoubleShot makes an unintrusive and highly intuitive addition to the table. Bets can be placed on the DoubleShot grid in straight up, split and corner positions and whenever the Billboard's "slot reel" matches the winning roulette number, players with DoubleShot bets covering that number win at the corresponding odds.

High odds and a familiar, roulette-centric design make DoubleShot and exciting yet respectful addition to the traditional game.
