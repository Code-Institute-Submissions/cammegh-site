Roulette Wheels

Having specialised in the manufacture of roulette wheels for 30 years, Cammegh has developed a deep knowledge of both the established and evolving demands of roulette. Roulette wheels must not only be examples of precision engineering, they must also be aesthetically beautiful, easy to operate, require minimal servicing and meet the increasing challenges to security.

Cammegh has led the industry in developing new materials and security measures to uphold the integrity of both the wheel and the game itself. The Mercury 360 RRS we manufacture, for example, makes use of a composite bowl to ensure a stable platform that is inert to changes in temperature and humidity, while the RRS component delivers random rotor speeds to combat wheel clockers.

Mercury 360

Cammegh's best-selling wheel, this product has all the technology to suit any security or gaming requirements you may have.

Mercury 360

The Mercury 360 is the industry's leading intelligent roulette wheel and Cammegh's flagship product. By elegantly fusing the unrivalled mechanical precision of the Connoisseur with the feature-packed Mercury 360 Game Engine, the Mercury 360 represents a quantum leap in manual roulette.

The Game Engine is a security-focused platform that constantly monitors rotor status, wheel level and ball characteristics throughout game play. Meanwhile a sophisticated array of four invisible In-Rim sensors provide secure winning number recognition and output live data for table displays, analysis tools, Electronic Gaming Terminals and online. Commbining this with Cammegh's Wheel Manager Application for Mercury 360, the game event log can be exported via the Ethernet port of the supplied B.O.B communications hub to a PC or network for data analysis and diagnostics.

As throughout the range, the Mercury 360 is customisable with a range of finishes and veneers. It is available with traditional or scalloped pockets, and in either single, double or even triple-zero number ring configurations.

Mercury 360 Mini

The Mercury 360 Mini is a perfect 3/4 scale recreation of the Mercury 360, including In-Rim sensors. The reduced size of the Mini's racetrack creates a tighter curvature that increases friction on the ball, causing it to drop faster and introducing a significant productivity advantage to the operator.

Ideally suited for stadium installations, the Mercury 360 Mini will deliver up to 120 games per hour, as compared to a full-size wheel's 70. The step-up in productivity comes with no impact on player comfort or betting time.

Mercury 360 RRS

Beat the cheats by giving the edge back to the house.

Mercury 360 RRS

Roulette has not changed in 200 years, but advances in modern technology have presented the traditional game with a new challenge.

The rise of smartphone apps designed to 'clock' the ball and rotor speeds has made it easier to identify a roulette wheel's winning and losing sectors, thus granting them a significant advantage over the house. To address this risk, the unique and patented Mercury 360 RRS includes a Random Rotor Speed feature that randomly and imperceptibly changes the rate of deceleration of the rotor from game to game by means of an innovative contactless system. This dramatically increases the wheel's unpredictability and provides a new layer of security against wheel clocking, whilst also boosting productivity in the online environment by allowing bets to be placed after the ball is spun.

This wheel looks identical to the standard Mercury 360 wheel. The RRS mechanism is totally invisible and inaudible. Since no part of the RRS mechanism comes into contact with the rotor, there is no wear and tear.

The graph here shows rotor deceleration during two test games on the same wheel, with RRS active. The rotor speeds are identical until 'Place Your Bets', at which point RRS begins to exert its contactless influence and imperceptibly slow down the rotors. The magnitude of the effect is randomised so no two games are the same. In the example, when the results are called the rotors have deviated by 19 and 25 pockets.

Mercury 360 Halo

The Mercury 360 Halo features a colourful LED band above the racetrack that dramatically illuminates the wheel. Single colours and smooth colour cycles can alternatively be used to accentuate game events, to denote table denominations or simply to further decorate the gaming floor.

The Halo band is available to all full-size wheels in the Mercury 360 family.

Slingshot 2

Elegant, accurate, reliable. The most authentic automatic stand alone roulette wheel available anywhere in the world.

Slingshot 2

The GLI-approved Slingshot 2 automatic roulette wheel launches the ball using a patented system of discreet air-jets, keeping it in view at all times to reassure players and uphold the integrity of the wheel. The elegant design combines bi-directional launch with random rotor and ball speeds, whilst also benefitting from Cammegh's unique RRS system (see the Mercury 360 RRS above), which imperceptibly and randomly changes the rotor speed to add an unprecidented layer of security.

The Slingshot 2 is mounted on top of a floor-standing frame, which houses the air compressor and is designed to fit inside an outer drum or 3rd party betting terminal carousel.

Slingshot 2 TT

Slingshot 2 TT

For flexibility, the Slingshot 2 is available in two variants. The standard model is mounted on top of a floor-standing frame, which houses the air compressor and is designed to fit inside an outer drum or 3rd party betting terminal carousel.

For installation on a standard roulette table or within a quieter environment, Cammegh provide the Slingshot 2 TT (Table Top). This wheel shares the principle design of its floor-standing sibling and allows the air compressor to be housed up to 30 metres away. This wheel also offers the option of a push-button launch, removing the possibility of dealer collusion.
