Roulette Wheels

Having specialised in the manufacture of roulette wheels for 30 years, Cammegh has developed a deep knowledge of both the established and evolving demands of roulette. Roulette wheels must not only be examples of precision engineering, they must also be aesthetically beautiful, easy to operate, require minimal servicing and meet the increasing challenges to security.

Cammegh has led the industry in developing new materials and security measures to uphold the integrity of both the wheel and the game itself. All of the wheels we manufacture make use of a composite bowl to ensure a stable platform that is inert to changes in temperature and humidity, while the RRS component of the Mercury 360 RRS delivers random rotor speeds to combat wheel clockers.
