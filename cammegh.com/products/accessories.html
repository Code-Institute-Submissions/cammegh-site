Accessories

Finish your casino setup with some of the fine accessories available below

Wheel Covers

Lockable Perspex wheel covers for Cammegh manual roulette wheels, incorporating security tags and Cammegh's unique locking mechanism, are available for both the standard Cammegh Trophy turret and the French Cammegh turret. These are fitted with felt pads to protect the top rim of the wheel they sit upon.

Wheel Levellers

The Cammegh Wheel Level has an elegant two-piece design for exceptional reliability. Magnetism holds the arms together and pushes them outwards, locking the device into the ball track so both spirit levels can be read.

This product is currently going under development work to improve the design, and as such the picutre shown (the current version) will soon be upgraded.

TD3 Keypads

The TD3 Keypad provides quick access to a number of Baccarat and Roulette functions, such as results entry, table limits and open/closed status. LED back lighting glows red or blue when a Baccarat result is entered.

Roulette Balls

High quality roulette balls in Ivorine (shown) and Teflon, with 18mm and 21mm sizes available.

Dollies

Cammegh is proud to now sell dollies that are perfect miniature replicas of the non-engraved standard Cammegh Trophy turret. These are available in nickel-plated as standard, with other finishes available upon request.

Gaming Chips

Cammegh's gaming chips are made with 4 Colour Processing, ensuring an exact finish across all chips within a batch. With embeddable security features available and sequential serial numbering from 1-1,000,000, these chips are compatible with any chiping machine.
