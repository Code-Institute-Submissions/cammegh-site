Cammegh Ltd, maker of The World's Finest Roulette Wheel, was established in 1989. The founder, Bill Cammegh, first arrived on the idea for the company after seeing a roulette wheel in action and thinking: "I can do better than that."


Cammegh now employs over 30 staff, who all share that same philosophy, and has built its reputation on quality products, high customisability and competitive pricing. The company not only makes fantastic roulette wheels, but has a strong team that build displays and design graphics for a range of casino uses. From fully customisable roulette layouts, to Baccarat, Side Bets and Punto Banco, Cammegh has the solution for you.
